<h3 class="my-2">Statement</h3>
    <p>
        Trivial one! So that you get used to the platform. Output the sum of two integers. Sounds easy enough, doesn`t it?
    </p>

    <hr>

    <h3 class="my-2">Input format</h3>
    <p>
        File adunare.in contains, on the first line, two numbers, A and B.
    </p>

    <hr>

    <h3 class="my-2">Output format</h3>
    <p>
        You should output one integer - the sum A+B !
    </p>

    <hr>

    <h3 class="my-2">Restrictions</h3>
    <p>
        1 <= A, B <= 10^18
    </p>

    <hr>
