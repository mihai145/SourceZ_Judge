<h3 class="my-2">Statement</h3>
    <p>
        Classic one! Given an array of length N, find the longest increasing subsequence!
    </p>

    <hr>

    <h3 class="my-2">Input format</h3>
    <p>
        File scm.in contains, on the first line, positive integer N, the size of the array.
    </p>
<p>
        On the second line, there are N integers, a[1], a[2], ... , a[N], the elements of the arrray.
    </p>

    <hr>

    <h3 class="my-2">Output format</h3>
    <p>
        You should output one integer - the length of the LIS!
    </p>

    <hr>

    <h3 class="my-2">Restrictions</h3>
    <p>
        1 <= N <= 10^5
    </p>
	<br>
	<p>
        -10^6 <= A[i] <= 10^6, i=1..N
    </p>

    <hr>
